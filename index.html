Epic Baby
How To Play:
You are a super baby with powers of super strength and super jump. Collect as many bottle as you can before reaching the lolipop at the end of the level without falling off the clouds. Avoid all vegetables.

Controls:

  • Press "Enter" key to start game
  • Up arrow to Jump
  • Left arrow to move Left
  • Right arrow to move Right
  • Press 'R' key to Restart game at anytime.
